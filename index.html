About Remo North

A Brief Overview

Remo North Local Government Area is a vibrant and historically rich part of Ogun State, Nigeria, known for its unique Yoruba heritage and strong community values. Home to towns like Isara, Ode-Remo, and Akaka-Remo, the area celebrates its deep-rooted traditions through annual festivals, arts, and cultural practices. With a mission to foster sustainable growth and improve the quality of life for all residents, the council is committed to transparency, community engagement, and progressive development.
